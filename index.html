Interstellar Zero

Minneapolis - Saint Paul, MN

Interstellar Zero is an alternative rock band from Minneapolis - Saint Paul, Minnesota. We create music that is both unique and accessible. Our style is a combination of space rock, electronic music, and improvisational influences. Our goal is to make music that is both enjoyable to listen to and meaningful.

We are currently working on an album, "Interstellar Zero". We have also performed in venues throughout Minnesota.

If you would like to learn more about us, or hear our music, email us at info@interstellarzero.com.
